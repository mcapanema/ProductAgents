# Market Intelligence — Authentication & Access

## Competitive landscape
- The top three competitors (Acme Identity, LoginHub, GateKeep) all ship native SSO/SAML on their mid-tier and enterprise plans.
- Two of them gate SSO behind the enterprise tier, using it as an upsell lever (the "SSO tax").

## Industry trends
- Enterprise buyers increasingly require SSO/SCIM in security questionnaires before procurement can proceed.
- Analyst reports cite SSO as table stakes for any B2B SaaS selling above $25k ACV.

## Recent launches / shifts
- LoginHub launched SCIM auto-provisioning last quarter and cited it as a win-rate driver in enterprise deals.
- A wave of SOC 2 / ISO 27001 adoption is pushing identity controls earlier in the buying cycle.
